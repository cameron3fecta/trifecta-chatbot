# Trifecta Project Process

Trifecta works with clients through a structured process designed to ensure successful outcomes.

## Discovery

The first step is understanding the client’s goals, audience, and challenges. This stage includes planning, research, and defining project scope.

## Strategy

Based on discovery insights, the team develops a strategy for the project including site architecture, messaging, and technology approach.

## Design

Design focuses on creating clear, engaging user experiences that align with the client’s brand and goals.

## Development

The development phase includes building the website or system, implementing content management systems, and integrating required features.

## Launch

After testing and quality assurance, the website or system is launched.

## Ongoing Support

Many clients continue working with Trifecta for ongoing website support, marketing, SEO, and system improvements.
